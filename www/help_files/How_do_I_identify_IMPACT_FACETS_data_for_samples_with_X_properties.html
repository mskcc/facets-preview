How do I identify IMPACT Facets Data for samples with X properties?

If you want to identify samples with specific properties, for example, samples of a specific cancer type or with whole genome duplication, contact Adam Price if you do not have access to the IMPACT repository on Terra/Juno/Iris.

If you do have access to the repository, the easiest way to interact and select samples is by using FacetsAPI, a powerful toolkit for interacting with FACETS repositories with extensive documentation and easy-to-implement examples.
